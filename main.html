Movie Summary

Interstellar is a science fiction film that follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new habitable planet for humanity. The movie explores themes of time, love, sacrifice, and survival beyond Earth.

Main Actors

The movie stars Matthew McConaughey as Cooper, Anne Hathaway as Dr. Amelia Brand, Jessica Chastain as Murph, and Michael Caine as Professor Brand.
